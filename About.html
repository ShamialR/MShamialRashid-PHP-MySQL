About Cinema Critique

Cinema Critique is a website I am designing for my Web Development Course at Bucks New Uni, currently I am working on the website alone as the programmer developer and designer. The website was created to give users a platform to view Cinema Critiques reviews on popular movies aswell as reviews from other popular sites such as imdb, aswell as posting trailers of the films to give our audience an all in one website to make our audiences movie finding experience much easier.
